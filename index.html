Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

how do you handle deliveries?

I will be using Pick Up Mtaani for Nairobi and its environs. For orders outside Nairobi, liasing with your preferred courier services is okay as well.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order through Instagram, or WhatsApp.

What is the delivery time?

Delivery time depends on your location. I strive to deliver within 5-7 business days.

Which products do you offer batch production?

Stickers, flyers, bookmarks and character cards. Keep in mind this requires a initial deposit.
